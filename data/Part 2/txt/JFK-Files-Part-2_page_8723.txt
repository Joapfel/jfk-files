OOA 3/215,189 dated 27 March 1962 
Concerns Fidel CASTRQf:s intelligence service that 
operated out of the 13th floor of the !NRA Bldg., in 
Havana. Appears that its mission was to infiltrate

OOA 3/215,192 dated 27 March 1962 
Concerns agents of Fidel CASTRO's intelligence service. 
Luis TACORNAL made several trips to the U.S. on missions 
for Fidel. TACOR.-W. told Juan ORTA he was recruited by 
Rolando MASFERRER to form resistance unit in Havana. 
ORTA told TACORNAL to carry out mission to see how many 
cotmterrevolutionaries he could get to join the 
resistance unit.
